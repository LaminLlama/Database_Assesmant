Celebrate Reading!

This page celebrates books and reading. Please browse through the various sections for a brief preview of some of my favorite books.

All artwork used on this page is Public Domain material (via Pixabay) {links to http://pixabay.com/ }.

Accessing reading material has never been easier, either visit your local library to get free books or, if you have a device like an iPad / tablet, use the OverDrive Media app to download books from your library's eCollection for free. {link: https://app.overdrive.com/ }

Please use the side bar to search for your local library. A full list of libraries in New Zealand can be found at the preceding link {links to https://natlib.govt.nz/directory-of-new-zealand-libraries}.
